
Story Narrative:
As a: student interested in economic trends
I want: to analyze market data, past and present,
So that: I can learn about market trends.
Acceptance Criteria:
1. Users should be able to view simple trends in market data.
2. Market data should be graphable against common economic assumptions and curves.
3. Data should editable to include custom labels in figures.
4. Our assumptions and strategy for analysis of the data should be accessible to users.
